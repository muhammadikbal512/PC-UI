About Us

Plant Culture is a culinary destination that presents an unrivaled Vegan Casual Fine Dining experience located in Alam Sutera. We are committed to delivering high quality food offerings, combining flavor innovation with the wisdom of the Nusantara flavors in each of our dishes.

Our vision is to elevate the local culture by serving delicious, healthy, environmentally friendly vegan food and providing a culinary experience that impresses the mind, body and taste buds.

Our mission is to provide Vegan food options that create happiness for each of our guests. We are committed to using high quality, organic and local ingredients wherever possible in each of our culinary creations.

Chef Axhiang LeeYan, a MasterChef Indonesia Season 4 alumni in 2015, is a talented culinary expert with extensive experience in fusing flavors and creativity in Vegan dishes. His unwavering dedication to the art of cooking makes him an excellent team leader at Plant Culture.
